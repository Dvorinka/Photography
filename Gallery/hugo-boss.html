Hugo BOSS Eau de Toilette

Hugo BOSS Eau de Toilette

1/3/2025

Detail

I shot the Hugo Boss perfume outdoors in a snowy setting, using a Sony Alpha A7 IV with a 50mm f/1.8 lens. I set the ISO to 400 and the shutter speed to 1/4000, focusing on close-ups of the bottle with a blurred snowy background for dramatic depth. Natural light emphasized the frosted elements and reflective surface. The style was minimalistic and commercial, aiming to showcase the product in a clean, premium way with a fresh, crisp vibe.
