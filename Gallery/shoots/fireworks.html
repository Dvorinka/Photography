New Year’s FIREWORKS

New Year’s FIREWORKS

12/31/2024

Detail

I captured New Year’s fireworks with my Sony Alpha A7 IV, using a 50mm Prime f/1.8 lens, without a tripod. I set the ISO to 1000 and adjusted the shutter speed between 1/25 and 1/160 to suit the low-light conditions. Despite not using a tripod, I aimed to capture the vibrant bursts of color against the dark sky with sharp details and beautiful light trails.
