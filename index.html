About Me

Hey there! I’m Tomas Dvorak, a storyteller with a camera, based in the Czech Republic. Ever since my Sony Alpha A7 IV became my trusty sidekick, I’ve been chasing the art of turning moments into visual masterpieces. My journey into photography began with a deep love for cinematic visuals. I aim to create images that feel like they belong on the silver screen, whether I’m capturing sweeping landscapes, intense sports action, or portraits that tell a story. My passion doesn’t stop with photography—I’m also diving into the world of cinematography, blending my skills to bring storytelling to life in new ways. Thanks for dropping by! Let’s make some magic together—whether it’s collaborating on a shoot or just geeking out over photography. Hit me up through the contact section or catch me on Instagram at @tdvorak_photos. Let’s create something unforgettable!
